The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Your Name

It has a stunning animation, emotionally resonant story about connection and memory, charming characters, and beautiful music.

The Last Holiday

This movie reminds us that Life is too precious to waste on Fear and Hesitation.It encourages us to embrace Every Moment,take Risk,and Live with courage and Authenticity

Hidden Figures

This movie conveys the message that determination,talent and courage can break down barries,regardless of race and gender.
